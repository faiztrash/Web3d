Travel Information

Helpful tips and visuals to get you ready for your next adventure.

Air Travel Essentials

Before heading to the airport, ensure you have your boarding pass, valid ID, and any travel documents. Most airlines recommend arriving early to allow enough time for check-in and security.

Inside the Airport

Many airports have automated kiosks and bag drops. Be ready for security checks — remove electronics and liquids as instructed.

Packing Smart

Choose a durable carry-on and pack essentials like a charger, travel pillow, and ID. Check your airline’s baggage rules in advance.
