What will this guide include?

This guide is designed to prepare you for your first holiday, ensuring that you will have a stress free holiday and have everything you need! I have prepared a series of models to help show you what everything you will need should look like, from how your luggage should look, to what your boarding pass might look like. I have also included a snack selector, which will allow you to select your favourite flavour of 3D Doritos! This is a fun way to get started with 3D models and see how they can be used in a real world application.
